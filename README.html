TIC TAC TOE GAME MECHANICS: Objective: Be the first player to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on a 3x3 grid. Setup: The game is played on a 3x3 grid. One player is "X" and the other is "O." Players take turns. Winning: The first player to get three marks in a row wins the game. Draw: If all nine squares are filled and no player has three marks in a row, the game ends in a draw. Optional: You can decide to play multiple rounds and keep score to see who wins the most games.
